About the Event

Why Commodity Markets Matter Now

In today's financial environment shaped by inflation cycles, global supply disruptions, and geopolitical risks, commodities are becoming an increasingly important asset class. COMCLAVE brings together industry experts, traders, institutions, and investors to discuss how commodity markets function and how they can complement traditional investments.

This summit is designed to help participants gain clarity, perspective, and structured knowledge about commodity markets.

What You Will Learn

5 Key Learning Areas

Gain structured knowledge across five critical areas of commodity market education, delivered by industry experts in a focused one-day programme.

Commodities as an Asset Class

  • Why commodities matter in diversified portfolios
  • Inflation protection through real asset exposure
  • Role of commodities alongside equities and bonds

Understanding Commodity Markets

  • Supply and demand dynamics
  • Global commodity cycles and macro drivers
  • Impact of geopolitical and environmental factors

Trading Instruments

  • Commodity futures and options
  • Exchange traded instruments
  • Understanding market participation

Technical Analysis in Commodity Trading

  • Reading price trends and charts
  • Key technical indicators
  • Combining technical insights with market fundamentals

Responsible Participation

  • Risk awareness in commodity trading
  • Understanding leverage
  • Building disciplined trading practices
